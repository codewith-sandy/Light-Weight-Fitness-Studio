Our Story

WHERE STRENGTH MEETS DISCIPLINE

At Light Weight Fitness Gym, we believe everyone deserves access to premium fitness facilities. Located in the heart of New Vellanur, we've built more than just a gym — we've created a community of warriors committed to transformation.

With state-of-the-art equipment, certified trainers, and an atmosphere that pushes you to your limits, we're here to help you achieve what you never thought possible.

Our journey began in 2021 with a simple vision: to make premium fitness accessible to everyone in New Vellanur and surrounding areas. Today, we're proud to be the most trusted gym in the region with over 500 active members.
